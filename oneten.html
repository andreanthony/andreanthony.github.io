OneTen is a coalition of leading executives who are coming together to upskill, hire and advance one million Black Americans over the next 10 years into family-sustaining jobs with opportunities for advancement.

_


We connect employers with talent partners and leading non-profits who support development of diverse talent. By creating more equitable and inclusive workforces, we believe we can reach our full potential as a nation of united citizens.

What is OneTen?
+

OneTen multiplies the power of existing and successful programs without replacing their efforts.

We’re creating a network of providers, from select local initiatives to employer-run programs, with the flexibility to meet and sustain the needs of a changing workforce.

We connect them with employers who are committed to hiring, upskilling and advancing Black talent.

OneTen manages the relationship between providers and employers to assure quality and long-term growth.

As an employer, we’ll work with you to hire, retain and advance Black talent, and develop inclusive company cultures that contribute to their success.

As a talent provider, you’ll be upskilling individuals for long-term growth and successful careers.
