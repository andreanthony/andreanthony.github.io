ABOUT

Cory is an entrepreneur, business leader, mentor, consultant, influencer and philanthropist. His work with BTEC, Founder Institute, and TIR Global Consultants has had impact locally (Chicago) and globally, and he has helped launch and scale more than a dozen companies, serving as a CEO, board member, and advisor to many of them. His expertise spans physical products, software, and business consulting. With over 20 years in the restaurant industry, Cory knew first-hand the pain points being hurdled daily and with that knowledge built a team to create ShedWool. With an intuitive method ShedWool saves it users the most time, resulting from shortcomings Cory had experienced as a manager using other company’s products. Cory’s superpower is creative optimism which is welcoming and draws other innovators to his circle. ⁣
